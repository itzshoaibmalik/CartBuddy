# 🛒 CartBuddy

**Your Smartest Shopping Companion!**

CartBuddy is a modern, user-friendly e-commerce platform built to enhance your online shopping experience. Whether you're buying daily essentials or the latest fashion, CartBuddy is your one-stop-shop with smart features, sleek design, and seamless navigation.

---

## 🌟 Features

- 🧠 **Smart Shopping Cart** — Intelligently remembers your preferences.
- ⚡ **Fast & Responsive UI** — Built for performance across all devices.
- 🔐 **Secure Checkout** — Safe payments with multiple gateways.
- 🔎 **Advanced Search & Filters** — Find what you need, instantly.
- 🎨 **Modern UI** — Clean, stylish, and intuitive design.
